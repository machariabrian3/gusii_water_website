Bowser services

Water delivery
GWASCO operates one water bowser dedicated to supplying clean water upon request and payment of the applicable service charge within Kisii, Nyamira counties, and surrounding areas. The water bowser offers a mobile water supply solution for locations without access to the water network or those underserved. For those interested in this service, our customer service section at GWASCO's main office can provide information regarding the availability and schedules of these trucks. Additionally, the company maintains a water loading point at the head office for customers equipped with their own means of transport.
