Gusii Water & Sanitation Co Ltd

The Gusii Water and Sanitation Company (GWASCO) is a water and sanitation service provider in the Kisii and Nyamira counties of Kenya. The company was established in 2006 and is responsible for the provision of water supply and sanitation services to the residents of the two counties.

Vision

To be the premier company in the provision of portable water and sanitation services in Kenya.

Mission

To provide safe water and sanitation services efficiently and effectively to sustainably meet expectations of the customers and other stakeholders.

Core Values
  • Customer focus
  • Integrity
  • Gender Responsiveness
  • Professionalism
  • Team work
  • Innovation
